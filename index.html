WOMEN'S POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE
KALAMASSERY

Government Women’s polytechnic college is the only women’s Polytechnic college in Ernakulam district, It was established in 1983 with the vision to empower women. Started with Just two branches, college now offers four programs in various diploma streams. Collage is functioning at the heart ofKalamssery Town which is the prominent education hub in Ernakulam district. Located at the meeting point of Seaport-airport road and NH 47, college helps in easy commute of students from all parts of the district.

COURSES WE OFFER

ARCHITECTURE

Vision:
The program aims to mould aspiring designers or engineers to become Entrepreneurs in building construction industry and also to assist Architects by giving exposure to various materials, technologies, softwares and services. It also aims to promote the students to pursue higher studies.
Mission:
* Support the students to achieve leadership qualities and excellence in academics by providing better learning environment and quality teaching
* Enhance students skill in sketching, drafting and designing of built environment.
* Empower girls to support themselves and their family by building up a career on their on.

COMPUTER ENGINEERING

Vision:
To mould qualified and skilled women professionals with innovative ideas and creative thinking who can excel in the competitive world of information technology. Mission:
* To provide a strong theoretical and practical background across the computer science discipline with emphasis on software development.
* To impart the skills necessary to continue education and grow professional.
* To inculcate professional behavior, ethical values, team spirit and leadership qualities.

ELECTRONICS

Vision:
To serve the society and moulding qualified and skilled women professionals with creativity, integrity, accountability and environmental concern , in tune with advanced technological revolution in Electronics and Communication Engg. Mission:
* To achieve academic excellence by structured teaching – learning process.
* To bridge the gap between classroom learning and practical implementation of technology.
* To facilitate lifelong learning and help students find the right career opportunities.
* To establish centre of excellence in thrust area to nurture the spirit of innovation and creativity among students.

COMMERCIAL PRACTICE

Vision:
The Programme aims to mould the students into professionals of international standards in office administration by giving ample exposure to accounting, communication, automation and documentation and also gears up aspiring students to pursue higher education. Mission:
* The programme supports the students through high quality teaching and practice that provide ample environment for intellectual thinking, ethical practices and overall development that empowers them greatly and makes them excel in secretarial services
* The programme instils in students the confidence to pursue successful careers by providing quality diploma education.
* Maintain strong relationship between the institution & the industry for student development.

Training & Placement Cell

Training & Placement cell is the backbone of any institution. From the very beginning this college lays greater emphasis on Industrial Training and Practical Training for Diploma students. The Placement Cell provides complete support to the visiting companies at every stage of placement process. Arrangements for Pre-Placement Talks, Written Tests, Interviews and Group Discussions are made as per the requirement of the visiting companies.
